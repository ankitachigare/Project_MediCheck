Frequently Asked Questions


A full blood test typically includes a variety of individual tests that help evaluate your overall health and diagnose certain medical conditions.
1.Complete Blood Count(CBC) : This test measures the number of red blood cells,white blood cells,and platelets in your blood.
2.Blood Chemistry Test:These tests assess your organ functions,electrolytes,blood sugar levels,cholesterol,and more.
3.Blood Glucose Test:Checks your blood sugar levels,which can help detect diabetes or pre-diabetes.
4.Vitamin and Mineral Levels: Can include Test for vitamin D,vitamin B12,and other essential nutrients.

Medical tests are essential because they provide critical insights into your health, often before symptoms appear. They help detect underlying conditions, monitor existing health issues, and guide treatment decisions. Early detection through regular testing can prevent complications and improve your quality of life. Think of them as a proactive step in maintaining your well-being, empowering you with the knowledge to make informed health choices. Simply put, medical tests aren't just about diagnosing—they're about protecting your future health.

At Medicheck, we offer a comprehensive range of tests designed to give you a clear, detailed picture of your health. Our blood tests and diagnostic reports include:
Complete Blood Count (CBC): Provides crucial insights into your blood cells, helping identify conditions like anemia or infections early.
Lipid Profile: Assesses your cholesterol levels to gauge your heart health and risk of cardiovascular diseases.
Blood Glucose Test: Monitors your blood sugar levels to help detect diabetes or pre-diabetes before symptoms appear.
Kidney and Liver Function Tests: Keep track of how well your kidneys and liver are functioning to prevent long-term damage.
Thyroid Function Test: Measures your thyroid hormones to ensure your metabolism, energy levels, and overall health are balanced.
Vitamin and Mineral Levels: Checks your essential nutrients like Vitamin D, B12, and more, helping you avoid deficiencies and maintain optimal health.
We also offer a range of specialized tests tailored to your specific needs, including pregnancy tests, allergy screenings, infection panels, and even cancer markers. At Medicheck, we don’t just focus on diagnosing—you get a roadmap to better health. Each test is a step toward a more informed and proactive approach to your well-being.
